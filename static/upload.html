How to Get These Files

To upload a class for avatar generation, you will need:

  • A Slides PDF or Google Slides ID/URL (optional if you already have the video).
  • An Audio narration file (.wav or other audio formats).
  • A Timestamps JSON file that indicates when each slide changes.
  • An Avatar image or video (optional).

Option 1: Generate Your Own Files

Use this Colab notebook to process your class recording and slides. Upload your Google Slides (or PDF) and an existing video of the class. The notebook will generate:

  • audio.wav
  • video.mp4
  • timestamps.json

You can then upload these here, or replace video.mp4 with the slideshow PDF.
